Whitepaper

CHAINESPORT NO GAMBLING, NO CASINO, NO BETS, NO LOTTERY! Only Fair Skill-Based Competitions where players, play against players! Not another token project. Not another speculative play. Executive Summary ChainEsport is creating the world’s first universal skill-based competition platform, built to redefine how players compete, earn, and connect in the modern gaming industry. Across Europe, more than 128 million players compete every day — yet fewer than 1 % ever earn anything for their time, talent, or dedication. The other 99 % play for passion alone — an enormous community ready for change. ChainEsport is building the infrastructure that will allow these millions of players to turn skill into real on-chain results, in a fully transparent and compliant way. The platform connects players from all major PvP titles — shooters, fighting games, sports, strategy... — across every platform: PC, console, and mobile. Through secure, non-custodial smart contracts, entry fees flow into verified reward pools, and winners are rewarded instantly in USDC, without speculation or tokens. Non-Custodial Escrow System ChainEsport is a non-custodial platform. Players always retain full control of their wallets. When entering a match, entry fees and prize pools are deposited directly into a smart contract escrow. Funds remain locked until the match is completed, after which the escrow contract automatically releases payouts according to the submitted results. This ensures that: Player funds are never in company custody. All flows are transparent and verifiable on-chain. Funds cannot be commingled with company reserves. Winners earn USDC instantly, directly to their wallets. ChainEsport applies a small 2% service fee on each competition entry. This fee supports platform operations, security, and infrastructure while ensuring a sustainable revenue stream for both the company and node Node Supporters. ChainEsport is not just a gaming project, this is the bridge that brings millions of gamers into crypto — through a real, sustainable use case.
